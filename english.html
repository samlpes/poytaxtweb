About Us

Our company was established in 2006, and for 16 years it has been actively involved in supplying and trading cardboard and paper products, mainly in the packaging and printing industries in Uzbekistan.

In 2014, we started sheeting production; today we sheet more than 500 tons of paper and board per month.

Our main competitive advantage is creating new trade channels in Uzbekistan for pulp and paper manufacturers. We represent the interests of Mondi Syktyvkar (Russia), Arkhangelsk APPM (Russia), KOTKAMILLS OY (Finland) in the territory of Uzbekistan.

New Product!!!
New ECO offset paper, from АО Group "ИЛИМ" production

Eco paper is a material that has gained quite a bit of popularity over the past decade. There were several reasons, among which are excellent performance characteristics, quality printing, and, of course, environmental friendliness.

This material is suitable for different printing methods, despite the fact that initially paper was created only for offset printing.

Advantages of Paper:
- No glare due to matte surface.
- The convenience of using offset paper.
- Choice of any density.
- Low cost.
