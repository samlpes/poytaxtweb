About Us

Our company was founded in 2006, and for 16 years has been actively engaged in the supply and trade of cardboard and paper products, mainly in the packaging and printing industry of Uzbekistan.

In 2014, we started the production of flare cutter; today we produce more than 500 tons of paper and board per month.

Our main competitive advantage is the creation of new trade channels in Uzbekistan for pulp and paper producers. We represent the interests of such large companies as: Mondi Syktyvkar (Russia), Arkhangelsk APPM (Russia), KOTKAMILLS OY (Finland) on the territory of Uzbekistan.
